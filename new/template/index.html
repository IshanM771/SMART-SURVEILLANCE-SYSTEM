    About Us

    “Guardians Watch” envisions a holistic approach that combines real-time video analysis with location tracking, enabling a proactive and precise security framework. By fusing these technologies, the project aims to enhance situational awareness, accelerate threat detection, and optimize resource allocation

    Our Mission

    Our Mission is to revolutionize conventional surveillance approaches by providing comprehensive situational awareness, accelerating threat detection, optimizing resource allocation, and ensuring a proactive and precise security framework

    Our Plan

    Growing demand for innovative solutions that leverage modern technologies to enhance security and monitoring capabilities.“Guardians Watch” aims to develop a comprehensive and intelligent smart surveillance system with location tracking
